A Haven for sweet memories

The Indulge Cake Haven UG is a cozy, purpose-driven bakery that serves freshly baked, handcrafted treats made with love. Every order — from our delightful cakes to our mouthwatering pastries — is crafted to bring joy, warmth, and exceptional taste to every bite. We bake beautiful, neat, delicious cakes for birthdays, weddings, anniversaries, baby and bridal showers, introductions and any special events made just the way you like them.

The Indulge Academy

At The Indulge Academy, we offer hands-on beginner baking classes designed to build your skills and confidence in the kitchen. Learn to bake a variety of cakes, pastries, and snacks in a fun, supportive environment — no experience needed! All equipment and ingredients are provided, and every student receives a certificate upon completion of the course.

Our Story

At The Indulge Cake Haven UG, we bake with passion, crafting a wide range of cakes, desserts, and pastries to satisfy every sweet craving. Our mission goes beyond just creating delicious treats; it's about making a meaningful impact in our community.

We are proud to donate 70% of our profits to the Benefaction Alms Foundation Uganda - a charity dedicated to uplifting underprivileged teenage mothers and vulnerable children. Through this foundation, we provide essential resources such as education, healthcare, and vocational training, empowering these young women to build a brighter future for themselves and their families.

Every cake you enjoy at our bakery is a step towards helping someone rewrite their story, transforming lives with each bite. By choosing The Indulge Cake Haven UG, you're not just treating yourself to something sweet - you're also contributing to a cause that spreads hope and opportunity to those who need it most.

THANK YOU FOR SUPPORTING OUR MISSION WITH EVERY INDULGENCE!

Visit Us

The Indulge BAF Outreaches

Through our partnership with Benefaction Alms Foundation (BAF), The Indulge Cake Haven UG dedicates 70% of our profits to empowering vulnerable teenage girls. Every last Sunday of every three months, we organize community outreaches where we offer basic baking and pastry skills training, provide starter kits (flour, sugar, baking tools) to help girls begin from home, offer mentorship, counselling, and emotional support, share meals, sanitary pads, and other essential supplies, and teach life skills and entrepreneurship to encourage self-reliance.

Our mission is to give every girl a second chance — with skills, confidence, and hope for a brighter future. Every cake you buy makes this possible.
